Vote Compass Israel Party Platform Comparison

About Vote Compass Israel

This tool summarizes and compares the published platforms and public positions of Israeli political parties. It is intended as a research aid for voters.

Platform positions are simplified summaries. Always consult each party's official platform before making decisions. This tool does not endorse any party or position.

Data is maintained in data.json — contributions and corrections are welcome via GitHub.
